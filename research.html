Research

Stuff I work on

1. Realistic modeling of Ru based water splitting catalysts

In this project, we use Density Functional Theory based molecular dynamics (DFT-MD) incorporating aqueous solvent in full molecular detail to gain fundamental understanding of highly effecient Ru based catalysts. More details can be found here.

2. Understanding effects of solvent in methanol dehydrogentation catalysts

In this project, we use Density Functional Theory based Molecular Dynamics incorporating aqueous solvent in full molecular detail to gain understanding on reaction mecahnisms of methanol dehydrogenation catalysts.

3. Implications of explicit solvent on volcano-activity and coordination-activity plots

In this project, we aim to analyze the effect of explicit solvation by means of dynamic calculations on the electrocatalytic activity of various transition metals for important reactions.
